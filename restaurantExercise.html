Making requests

1- Listen the audio and complete with the missing words: Can I, Could I or I would like/I'd like.

Waitress: Hello, are you ready to order?
Man: I am. Are you?
Woman: Yes, I think so. What is the soup of the day, please?
Waitress: It’s spicy carrot soup.
Woman: Hm, no. I don’t want that. I’ll start with the mushrooms, please. And after that, I have the chicken?
Waitress: Sure and potatoes chips or rice with your chicken?
Woman: I’ll have rice, please. Potatoes and chips are too heavy.
Waitress: Fine and you, sir?
Man: Hm, the tomato and bean salad to start, please.
Waitress: Sure. And for your main course?
Man: The bacon burger, please. I have it without cheese?
Waitress: No cheese. Sure. And what to drink?
Woman: A beer for me, please.
Man: Really? You usually have wine.
Woman: That’s true, but I don’t want that today.
Man: Hm, I’ll have a beer too, and we have some water too?
Waitress: Sure. I’ll bring your drinks in a moment.
Waitress: How was your meal?
Woman: Delicious. Thank you.
Waitress: dessert? The menu is on the wall over there.
Woman: Yes, I’d love one. But, hm, I can’t decide. The white chocolate cake or the orange cheesecake.
Man: Well, I know what I want: an apple pie.
Waitress: Sure. With cream or ice cream?
Man: Ice cream, please.
Waitress: No problem. And have you decided?
Woman: Well, I had orange cheesecake last time I was here, so I’ll have the white chocolate cake this time.
Waitress: Great, I’ll bring them for you soon.
