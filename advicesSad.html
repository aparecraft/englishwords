If you're sad with your English, you can:

Learn basic things

You cannot decorate a room without a house, so you need to have solid foundations. Learn the basics of English and practice until you master it.

Practice Regularly

Consistent practice is crucial for language improvement. Engage in daily activities such as reading books, articles, or news in English, watching your favorite movies or TV shows, and having conversations with native speakers if possible.

Immerse Yourself

You need to live the language you are learning! So immerse yourself as much as you can. Try to be an active leaner: write and speak as much as you can in English. You can make English your primary language for your personal writing. Example: create to-do lists or shopping lists in English!

Have simple goals

You do not need to aim to read an 800-page book every day. Try to create simple goals that make sense to you. Maybe to learn five new words every day or to speak English for an hour a week.
